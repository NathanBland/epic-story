Departure -

This is typically the part of the story that removes the hero from the world that they are familiar with, and casts them into stage two. To see some ideas on making your own, click the pencil above to edit your version.

Initiation -

Usually this section of the story will contain elements that train the hero The way that this is done can vary, and there are some suggestions for what each section might contain once you click edit.

The Return -

The Return is the portion of the story that frequently occurs right after, or during the final quest. As with the other sections, there are more details, suggestions, and ideas inside of the edit page.
